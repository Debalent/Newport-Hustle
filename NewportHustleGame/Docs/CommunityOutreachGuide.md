# Newport Community Outreach Guide

## Planning Framework for Authentic Community Representation

### Overview

This guide outlines our **planned approach** for respectfully engaging with the Newport, Arkansas community should this concept project move to actual development and release. Currently, Newport Hustle exists as a **technical demonstration** and **portfolio project** showcasing game development capabilities.

**Current Status**: Concept and technical proof-of-concept  
**Next Phase**: Community outreach and permission gathering  
**Goal**: Free community game with authentic representation

---

## Phase 1: Community Introduction

### Initial Outreach Strategy

#### 1. Local Media Engagement

- Contact Newport Independent newspaper
- Reach out to local radio stations
- Social media posts in Newport community groups
- Flyers at community bulletin boards

#### 2. Community Leaders First

- Mayor's office introduction
- City Council presentation
- Chamber of Commerce meeting
- Local business association outreach

#### 3. Community Gathering Points

- Newport Public Library
- Local coffee shops and restaurants
- Community center events
- Church congregations (with permission)
- Senior centers
- School district (for appropriate community events)

### Introduction Message Template

```text
Subject: "Newport Hustle" - Mobile Game Celebrating Our Community

Dear [Name/Organization],

We're developing a mobile game called "Newport Hustle" that celebrates the spirit and character of Newport, Arkansas. Our goal is to create an authentic, positive representation of our community that showcases the values of friendship, growth, and small-town pride.

We're reaching out to community members to see if anyone would be interested in having a character inspired by them included in the game. All participation is completely voluntary, and every character would be portrayed in a positive, respectful manner.

We believe Newport has incredible people with amazing stories, and we'd love to honor that in our game while being completely respectful of privacy and personal preferences.

Would you be interested in learning more about this project? We have detailed information about how characters would be represented and a comprehensive consent process.

Thank you for your time, and thank you for being part of what makes Newport special.

Best regards,
Newport Hustle Development Team
```

## Phase 3: Consent Collection

### Consent Form Distribution Strategy

#### 1. Direct Distribution

- Hand-delivered forms with personal explanation
- Community event booths and information tables
- Trusted community leader endorsements
- Local business partnerships for distribution
- Door-to-door outreach in appropriate areas

#### 2. Community Event Setup

- Information booth at Newport festivals
- Presentation at community organization meetings
- Library and community center partnerships
- Local business collaboration
- Church and civic group presentations

#### 3. Digital Options

- QR codes linking to online consent forms
- Social media information campaigns
- Email distribution through community networks
- Website with downloadable forms
- Online submission with confirmation system

### Documentation Requirements

#### 1. Consent Form Management

- Unique ID for each consent form
- Secure storage and backup systems
- Privacy protection protocols
- Regular audit and verification procedures
- Clear retention and disposal guidelines

#### 2. Character Development Records

- Character description documentation
- Community input integration tracking
- Development milestone records
- Revision history and approval notes
- Final implementation confirmation

#### 3. Privacy Protection

- Separate storage of personal and character data
- Limited access authorization protocols
- Regular security audits and updates
- Data breach response procedures
- Participant notification systems

## Phase 4: Character Development

### Character Creation Process

#### 1. Initial Character Design

- Create character based on consent form input
- Develop positive personality traits and background
- Design appropriate appearance and characteristics
- Establish meaningful role within game narrative
- Prepare detailed description for participant review

#### 2. Community Review Process

- Send character description to participant for approval
- Allow time for feedback and revision requests
- Implement requested changes promptly and thoroughly
- Confirm final approval before game implementation
- Maintain open communication throughout process

#### 3. Implementation Standards

- Emphasize positive character traits and values
- Ensure respectful dialogue and interactions
- Maintain authentic cultural representation
- Integrate educational value where appropriate
- Prioritize community benefit in all decisions

### Character Type Guidelines

#### Community Leaders

- Business owners and entrepreneurs
- Educational professionals and mentors
- Religious and spiritual community guides
- Civic leaders and community volunteers
- Healthcare and social service providers

#### Everyday Heroes

- Helpful neighbors and community friends
- Hardworking family members and parents
- Community volunteers and event organizers
- Local artisans and skilled craftspeople
- Youth leaders and student mentors

#### Cultural Representatives

- Long-time Newport families and residents
- Cultural tradition keepers and historians
- Local storytellers and community historians
- Community event organizers and leaders
- Artistic and creative community members

## Conclusion
