# Newport Hustle - Mobile GTA-Style Adventure Game

Welcome to **Newport Hustle**, a mobile adventure game set in the charming small town of Newport, Arkansas. Experience the authentic atmosphere of a White River community as you build your reputation, develop relationships, and create your own story in this GTA-inspired mobile experience.

## 🏞️ About Newport Hustle

Newport Hustle combines the open-world exploration and character-driven gameplay of GTA with the heart and soul of small-town Arkansas. Set in a lovingly recreated version of Newport, Arkansas, players return to their hometown to rebuild their life, earn respect, and become a valued member of the community.

### Key Features

- **Authentic Arkansas Setting**: Explore real Newport, AR street names including Main Street, Front Street, Hazel Street, and areas like Diaz and Jacksonport
- **Character-Driven Storytelling**: Deep relationships with memorable NPCs representing the heart of small-town community
- **Mobile-Optimized Gameplay**: Touch controls, mobile UI, and gameplay designed specifically for mobile devices
- **Business Simulation**: Run barbershops, partner with spas, and build your reputation in the local economy
- **Community Respect System**: Your actions affect how the entire community views and treats you
- **Real River Activities**: Fish the White River, explore waterfront areas, and enjoy Arkansas outdoor culture
- **Dynamic Weather & Time**: Experience Arkansas seasons and weather patterns that affect gameplay

## 🎮 Gameplay Overview

### Core Gameplay Loop
1. **Explore Newport**: Walk the real streets of Newport, from downtown to the riverfront
2. **Build Relationships**: Meet and interact with unique NPCs, each with their own stories and goals
3. **Develop Skills**: Master barbering, business management, and social skills
4. **Complete Missions**: Engage in story missions that explore themes of homecoming, community, and personal growth
5. **Make Choices**: Your decisions affect your reputation, relationships, and the story's direction

### Mission Structure
- **Main Story**: 4 major missions following your return to Newport and integration into the community
- **Side Quests**: Various optional missions that deepen relationships and explore Newport's culture
- **Dynamic Events**: Random events that test your values and affect your standing in the community

## 🗺️ Newport, Arkansas - The Setting

Our game faithfully recreates the geography and atmosphere of Newport, Arkansas:

### Real Locations Featured
- **Downtown Newport**: Historic Main Street with traditional businesses
- **White River Waterfront**: Beautiful riverside areas with docks, fishing spots, and marinas
- **Barber's Row**: Traditional barbershops representing Newport's service industry heritage
- **Spa District**: Modern wellness businesses bringing contemporary amenities to Newport
- **Residential Areas**: Tree-lined neighborhoods with family homes
- **Diaz District**: Cultural area representing Newport's diverse community
- **Jacksonport Area**: Historic region with Civil War heritage and state park

### Real Streets Included
- Main Street (Historic downtown backbone)
- Front Street (Waterfront commerce)
- Hazel Street (Residential charm)
- Walnut Street (Community services)
- Highway 67 (Major thoroughfare)
- River Road (Scenic riverside drive)
- And many more authentic Newport streets!

## 🎯 Game Philosophy

### Respect Over Violence
Unlike traditional GTA games, Newport Hustle focuses on:
- Building community relationships rather than conflict
- Earning respect through positive contributions
- Solving problems through dialogue and cooperation
- Celebrating small-town values and community spirit

### Authentic Cultural Representation
- Respectful portrayal of Arkansas culture and values
- Accurate geographic representation of Newport
- Celebration of small-town American life
- Emphasis on community, family, and belonging

## 🚀 Getting Started

### For Players
1. Download Newport Hustle from your mobile app store
2. Create your character using the Newport-inspired customization system
3. Begin your journey back to Newport with Mission 01: "Welcome Back"
4. Explore, interact, and build your place in the community!

### For Developers
1. Clone the repository
2. Open in Unity 2021.3 LTS or later
3. Configure mobile build settings for your target platform
4. Review the documentation in the `Docs/` folder
5. Check the Config files for game balance and content
