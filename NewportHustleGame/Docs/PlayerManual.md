## Getting Started

### Welcome to Newport, Arkansas

Newport Hustle combines the excitement of GTA-style gameplay with authentic representation of Newport, Arkansas. Drive through real streets, visit actual local businesses, and connect with characters inspired by the Newport community.

### Game Objectives

- **Build Relationships**: Connect with Newport's community members
- **Explore Authentic Locations**: Visit Jordan's Gas Station, Lackey Tamale Shop, and The Yella Store
- **Master Vehicle Mechanics**: Drive cars, SUVs, motorcycles through Newport streets
- **Navigate Police Interactions**: Understand the wanted system and law enforcement
- **Complete Story Missions**: Experience narrative celebrating Newport culture
- **Customize Your Experience**: Modify vehicles and create your unique Newport story

## Mobile Controls

### Primary Controls

#### Vehicle Controls

- **Accelerate**: Swipe up from center of screen or use accelerator button
- **Brake/Reverse**: Swipe down or use brake button
- **Steering**: Tilt device (gyroscope) OR touch left/right sides of screen
- **Handbrake**: Quick swipe down or dedicated button
- **Exit Vehicle**: Tap exit button when stopped

#### On-Foot Controls

- **Movement**: Virtual joystick (bottom-left)
- **Run**: Hold sprint button while moving
- **Interact**: Tap interaction button near objects/people
- **Enter Vehicle**: Approach and tap enter button
- **Camera**: Pinch to zoom, drag to rotate view

#### Universal Controls

- **Map**: Tap map button (top-right)
- **Menu**: Three-line menu button (top-left)
- **Inventory**: Backpack icon
- **Settings**: Gear icon in main menu

### Desktop Controls (Alternative)

- **WASD**: Movement/Vehicle control
- **Mouse**: Camera control and steering assist
- **Space**: Handbrake
- **F**: Enter/Exit vehicles
- **M**: Map
- **ESC**: Main menu
- **E**: Interact with objects/people
- **Shift**: Sprint/Boost
- **Tab**: Switch camera views
- **Enter**: Chat/dialogue
- **R**: Restart current mission
- **P**: Pause game

### Vehicle Mechanics

#### Entering & Exiting Vehicles

1. **Approach any vehicle** (look for interaction prompt)
2. **Tap "Enter" button** or press F (desktop)
3. **Wait for entry animation** (varies by vehicle type)
4. **To exit**: Come to complete stop, tap "Exit" button
5. **Emergency exit**: Available during slow speeds (causes minor damage)

#### Vehicle Damage System

- **Health Bar**: Displayed in bottom-left during driving
- **Visual Damage**: Scratches, dents, broken windows appear gradually
- **Performance Impact**: Damaged vehicles handle worse, slower acceleration
- **Repair Options**: Visit Jordan's Gas Station or other service stations
- **Total Damage**: Vehicle will smoke and eventually stop functioning

#### Fuel System

- **Fuel Gauge**: Next to speedometer
- **Consumption**: Varies by vehicle type and driving style
- **Refueling**: Available at Jordan's Gas Station and other fuel stops
- **Low Fuel Warning**: Gauge turns red, warning sounds
- **Empty Tank**: Vehicle gradually loses power and stops

## Police System

### Understanding the Wanted System

The Newport Police Department responds realistically to player actions with a 4-level wanted system.

#### Wanted Level 1 ⭐

- **Trigger**: Minor violations (speeding, running red lights)
- **Response**: Single patrol car, routine traffic stop attempt
- **Duration**: 30-60 seconds
- **Escape**: Stop and comply, or drive carefully until stars fade
- **Penalty**: Small fine if caught

#### Wanted Level 2 ⭐⭐

- **Trigger**: Reckless driving, property damage
- **Response**: 2-3 patrol cars, active pursuit
- **Duration**: 1-2 minutes
- **Escape**: Evade for extended period or find hiding spots
- **Penalty**: Moderate fine, temporary vehicle impound

#### Wanted Level 3 ⭐⭐⭐

- **Trigger**: Hit and run, fleeing police
- **Response**: Multiple units, roadblocks, helicopter
- **Duration**: 3-5 minutes
- **Escape**: Requires skill, planning, and luck
- **Penalty**: Significant fine, longer impound period

#### Wanted Level 4 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

- **Trigger**: Prolonged chase, multiple violations
- **Response**: County-wide manhunt, spike strips, tactical units
- **Duration**: Until caught or successfully evaded
- **Escape**: Extremely difficult, requires expert evasion
- **Penalty**: Major consequences, potential game over

### Police Vehicles

- **Newport Patrol Unit** (Crown Victoria Police Interceptor)
  - High acceleration and top speed
  - Excellent ramming capability
  - Persistent pursuit behavior

- **Sheriff's Cruiser** (Grand Marquis)
  - Balanced performance
  - Used for roadblocks and backup
  - Professional pursuit tactics

### Avoiding Police Trouble

#### Legal Methods

1. **Pay Attention to Speed Limits**
   - Residential areas: 25 mph
   - Main streets: 35 mph
   - Highway sections: 55 mph

2. **Follow Traffic Rules**
   - Stop at red lights and stop signs
   - Use proper lanes
   - Avoid reckless driving near schools and churches

3. **Respect Community Events**
   - Slow down during festivals
   - Avoid disrupting community gatherings
   - Be courteous to pedestrians

#### Evasion Tactics

- **Break Line of Sight**: Use buildings and terrain
- **Change Vehicles**: Abandon hot vehicle, steal new one
- **Use Back Roads**: Avoid main streets during pursuit
- **Hide in Parking Areas**: Behind buildings, in alleyways
- **Water Crossings**: Some areas inaccessible to police cars
- **Motorcycle Advantage**: Use narrow spaces cars cannot follow
